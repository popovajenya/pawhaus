Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

We want you and your dog to be completely happy with your PAWHAUS purchase. If you're not satisfied, we're here to help.

Returns

You have 30 calendar days to return an item from the date you received it. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, in the same condition you received it, and in the original packaging.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned: personalized or custom-engraved items, opened food or treat products, items marked as final sale.

Refund Process

Once we receive your item, we will inspect it and notify you of the status of your refund. If approved, we will initiate a refund to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item for a different size or color, please contact us at returns@pawhaus.com. We'll do our best to accommodate your request.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you received a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage at support@pawhaus.com. We will send a replacement at no charge.

Shipping Costs

You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item arrived damaged or defective. Original shipping fees are non-refundable.

Contact

For any questions about returns, please email us at returns@pawhaus.com
