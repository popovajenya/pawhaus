Shipping Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

We want your order to arrive quickly and safely. Here's everything you need to know about how we ship.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (Monday through Friday, excluding holidays). You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has shipped.

Shipping Rates & Delivery Times

Shipping MethodEstimated DeliveryCost
Standard Shipping5-7 business days$4.99 (Free over $35)
Expedited Shipping2-3 business days$9.99
Overnight Shipping1 business day$19.99

Free Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $35. The discount is applied automatically at checkout.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on the carrier's website.

International Shipping

We currently ship within the United States only. International shipping is not available at this time. We hope to expand our shipping options in the future.

Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package arrives damaged or is lost in transit, please contact us at support@pawhaus.com within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue and send a replacement if necessary.

Contact

Questions about your shipment? Email us at support@pawhaus.com
